About Us

At Nguku Farm, we pride ourselves on raising our chickens in the most natural and humane way possible. Our hens are free to roam the lush, green pastures, eating a diet of organic grains, ensuring our eggs are both delicious and nutritious. Our mission is to provide our local community with the freshest eggs straight from the farm to your table.
